Hi! I'm currently a PhD candidate in computer science at CU-Boulder advised by James Martin. While here, I've interned at Bloomberg and Atilika. Previously, I graduated from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln with a B.S. in Mathematics.

I'm interested in verbs and roles in natural language processing and analyzing their usage by both people and computers. I also work with different graph structures of language, including ontologies and abstract meaning representations.

I can be reached at elizabeth.spaulding at colorado.edu.
